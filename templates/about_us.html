ABOUT US

We are a team of engineers, passionate about technology and keen to use our knowledge for the betterment of others. We have observed that there are several cases of pathologically affected voice that goes undetected and results in permanent damage or complete loss of voice. Also, as compared to other fields in the medical domain, voice pathology has received less attention.

With the voice being a principal tool allowing individuals to communicate, it is necessary to shed light as well as resolve this problem. Therefore, we have developed a tool to help out these individuals through early detection of their voice disorder.
